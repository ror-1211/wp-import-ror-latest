Integra Ledger wants to use blockchain as a trust layer and data integrity layer. Real Estate is an ocean of legal documents. They want to blockchain real estate meta data - identity as well management of physical assets. There are many actors in the real estate ecosystem - owners, suppliers managers. The distributed, trusted nature of a blockchain ledger let's all participants access and trust data.
