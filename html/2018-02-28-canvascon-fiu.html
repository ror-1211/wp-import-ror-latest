We've had the privilege of working with Instructure for many years building LTI tools and integrating with the Canvas API. Atomic Jolt has been a Canvas Alliance Partner for a number of years which means that we work closely with development teams at Instructure, build cool products to enhance Canvas and we go to Instructure's conferences. We've been at InstructureCon the past few years, and we've finally started sponsoring the regional CanvasCon conferences. That gave me the opportunity this week to join Canvas users and partners in Miami at Florida International University to show of our products:

  • Atomic Search
  • Atomic Learnosity Connector
  • Atomic CMS
  • Atomic Curriculum Tools
  • Atomic Assessments
