I am not sure how many years Jones soda has been doing this, but for a limited time during the holiday season you can get a variety of drinks.  Brussels Sprout with Prosciutto, Cranberry Sauce, Turkey & Gravy, Wild Herb Stuffing, and Pumpkin Pie are all included in their national pack.  There is a regional pack available in limited areas that comes with smoked salmon pate.  Enjoy.
